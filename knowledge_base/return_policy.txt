Kutral Return & Refund Policy

We stand behind the quality of our smoked chili paste. If you're not completely satisfied, here’s what you need to know:

Eligibility:
Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase if the jar is unopened and in its original packaging.

Process:
To initiate a return, email us at support@kutralfoods.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll issue a prepaid return label.

Refunds:
Refunds are processed within 7 business days after we receive the returned item. Refunds will be applied to the original payment method.

Exchanges:
We do not offer direct exchanges. Please return your item for a refund and place a new order if needed.

Shipping costs:
Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the product arrived damaged or defective.

Damaged items:
If your jar arrived broken or leaking, please email a photo of the damage along with your order number. We’ll send a replacement free of charge.
